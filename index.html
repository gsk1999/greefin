🤖 Scan Preferences
Enhanced Item Lookup
After scanning, uses web search to identify full product names from receipt shortforms (e.g. HNTS PNTBTR 340G → Hunts Peanut Butter 340g). Best for grocery receipts. Adds ~5–10s and uses slightly more API credits.
Verify Item Names Online
After scanning, searches the web to verify and correct product names — especially useful for thermal receipts with smudged or misread text (e.g. 99 Speedmart, Giant). Runs alongside Enhanced Lookup if both are on. Adds ~5–8s per scan.
Data Cleanup
Scans your entries for ones that don't look like real transactions — e.g. a stray header row accidentally pulled in from the Sheet (missing/invalid date or amount). Shows a preview before deleting anything.
⚠️ Recovery Tools
Rebuild Sheet from App — if your Sheet is messy, first clear its data rows manually (keep headers), then push everything from the app as fresh rows.
Clear Local Data & Re-sync from Sheet — use on a device with corrupted/stale local data (e.g. header rows appearing as entries, or entries out of sync with another device). Wipes this device's local copy and pulls fresh from the Sheet. Your Sheet data is NOT affected.
Google Sheets Sync (Cross-Device)
Connect to sync entries and settings to your "Daily Expenses" Google Sheet. This makes your data available on any device — phone, PC, anywhere.
